Why BNP is the GOAT.

I support BNP for their liberal, secular, and centrist stance. I believe in rational, progressive politics, and BNP embodies that vision right now. Tarique Rahman's speeches, especially after August 5, have consistently inspired me. His vision for the nation resonates with a future built on growth and fairness.

BNP's 31-point demand for a better Bangladesh shows their commitment to structured, meaningful change. Unlike others who manipulate people's emotions, BNP takes a rational approach to politics. When some groups wanted to ban Prothom Alo, The Daily Star, Somoy TV, BNP stood up for press freedom.

When there were calls to remove the President, BNP recognized the risks of creating a power vacuum and opposed it to maintain the country's stability. Similarly, when others wanted to bury the entire Constitution, BNP stood firm. They understood that reform is essential, but the Constitution is the foundation of our democracy and must be preserved. BNP is committed to changing only the necessary elements within the Constitutions, but burying or rewriting the entire document is simply unacceptable.

Even when there were demandsa to ban BAL, BNP said, "It's the people's decision. Let's have a vote - the people will decide." That rational, logical stance is why I support BNP. And I'll continue supporting them as long as they stay true to these principles. However, once they are in power, I will hold them accountable and criticize any wrongdoings for the good of the nation.
